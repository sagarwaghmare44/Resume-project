Tours and Activities

Hot-Air-Ballooning-in-Lonavala

Hot air ballooning in Lonavala offers a serene experience, soaring above lush landscapes. The rides last about 60 minutes, showcasing stunning views of valleys and hills. Safety is a priority, with experienced pilots guiding the balloons. Booking in advance is recommended, especially during peak seasons. Costs vary depending on the provider and season. It's an ideal activity for tourists and adventure enthusiasts. The experience provides a unique perspective of Lonavala's natural beauty. Overall, it's a memorable way to enjoy the region's scenic wonders.

Rating: 4.5 stars
